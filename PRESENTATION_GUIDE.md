## üìä EPIC Framework Structure

### E - EXPLAIN (2 minutes)
**Business Context & Data Overview**
- Enterprise: Banking & Financial Services
- Challenge: Optimize marketing effectiveness while managing credit risk
- Data Sources: 4,521 marketing records + 30,000 credit records
- Key Metrics: Conversion rates, default rates, customer segmentation

**Key Talking Points:**
- "We're analyzing banking enterprise data to improve decision-making"
- "Two critical datasets: marketing campaigns and credit risk"
- "Our goal is data-driven insights for strategic advantage"

### P - PROBLEM (2 minutes)
**Identified Business Challenges**
- Marketing efficiency gap: 88.5% non-conversion rate
- Credit risk exposure: 22.1% default rate
- Resource allocation challenges
- Seasonal performance variations

**Key Talking Points:**
- "Marketing campaigns have low conversion efficiency"
- "Credit portfolio shows significant default risk"
- "Need better targeting and risk assessment"

### I - INSIGHT (3 minutes)
**Data-Driven Discoveries**
- Retired customers: 23.5% conversion (2x average)
- Cellular contact: 14.4% conversion (62% improvement)
- October campaigns: 46.2% conversion (4x average)
- Age-based risk stratification opportunities

**Interactive Features to Highlight:**
- Use filters to show different customer segments
- Demonstrate seasonal patterns with monthly data
- Show risk heatmaps for different age/utilization combinations

### C - CONCLUSION (3 minutes)
**Strategic Recommendations & ROI**
- Immediate actions (0-30 days): Target retired segments, cellular-first strategy
- Short-term initiatives (1-3 months): October acceleration, risk monitoring
- Long-term strategy (3-12 months): Predictive models, integrated systems

**Financial Impact:**
- Total projected impact: +$5.0M annually
- Implementation investment: $1.15M
- ROI timeline: 3.5 months
- Break-even: Month 3
